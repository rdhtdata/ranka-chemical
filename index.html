Who We Are

About Ranka Chemical

Ranka Chemical was founded by Mr. Aivanth N. Ranka and Rushika Mehta with a clear vision: to build a highly efficient distribution bridge between chemical manufacturers and the scientific community.

As an authorized stockist for SD Fine Chem, we serve a diverse and prestigious clientele. Our network actively supplies high-quality chemicals to pharmaceutical companies, various manufacturing industries, research and development (R&D) laboratories, and universities.

Backed by Mr. Aivanth N. Ranka’s decade-long expertise in the chemical trading sector, we are transforming our operations into a true one-stop solution. By bringing premium laboratory instruments, labware, glassware, and chemicals together under one roof, we ensure that our partners in industry, research, and academia have everything they need to succeed.
